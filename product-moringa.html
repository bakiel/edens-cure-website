Effortless daily nourishment

Moringa has a mild, slightly earthy flavour that blends easily. It works in nearly any food or drink. Consistent daily use over 4+ weeks is when most people notice the biggest changes in energy, skin, and focus.

  1. 1
    Green Smoothie
    Blend 1 teaspoon into any smoothie — banana, mango, pineapple, or berry-based. The fruit sweetness balances the earthy notes perfectly.
  2. 2
    Morning Water
    Stir ½–1 teaspoon into warm (not boiling) water with lemon and honey. Drink first thing in the morning for maximum absorption.
  3. 3
    Food Supplement
    Sprinkle over salads, stir into soups after cooking, or add to pap or porridge. 1–2 teaspoons per serving is ideal.
  4. 4
    Dose Guide
    Start with ½ teaspoon daily for the first week to let your body adjust. Increase to 1–2 teaspoons daily from week 2. Safe for long-term daily use.
