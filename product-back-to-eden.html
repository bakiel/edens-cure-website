Daily ritual, simple as nature

Back to Eden is versatile. Use it as a daily tonic, add it to food, or blend it into smoothies. Consistent use over 30+ days produces the most significant results.

  1. 1
    Morning Tonic
    Mix ½ teaspoon into warm water or juice on an empty stomach, 15 minutes before breakfast. This is the most potent way to take it.
  2. 2
    Add to Food
    Stir into stews, soups, or rice dishes. Heat does not significantly reduce potency — the active compounds are heat-stable.
  3. 3
    Smoothie Blend
    Add 1 teaspoon to your morning smoothie with banana, mango, or almond milk to balance the spice and maximise absorption.
  4. 4
    Be Consistent
    Use daily for at least 30 days. Plant medicine works cumulatively — the longer you use it, the more your body responds.
