Revered across three millennia of healing

Nigella Sativa — known as Black Seed, Kammon, or Habbatus Sauda — has been used as medicine since the time of ancient Egypt and is one of the most documented healing seeds in the world. The active compound Thymoquinone (TQ) is what modern science believes gives it much of its extraordinary potency.

Our Kammon Black Seed is ground from whole seeds, preserving the full-spectrum volatile oils, omega fatty acids, and TQ content. Nothing extracted. Nothing added.

Small seed. Powerful protocol.

Black Seed has a peppery, slightly bitter taste — it works best when taken directly or mixed with something sweet like honey. Consistency is key: 90 days of daily use is considered a full treatment cycle in traditional medicine.

  1. 1
    Honey Mix (Classic)
    Mix ½ teaspoon into a tablespoon of raw honey. Take on an empty stomach in the morning. This is the most traditional and effective method.
  2. 2
    Warm Water Tonic
    Stir ½ teaspoon into warm water with lemon and a teaspoon of honey. Drink slowly, ideally 20 minutes before eating.
  3. 3
    Direct Consumption
    Place ½ teaspoon on the tongue and swallow with water. Traditional practitioners recommend this for maximum potency — the taste is intense but effective.
  4. 4
    Dosage
    ½–1 teaspoon daily. Not recommended for pregnant women without medical guidance. Safe for children over 12 at half the adult dose.

Over 650 peer-reviewed studies published since 2000

Nigella Sativa is among the most studied plants in pharmacognosy, with a remarkable breadth of documented effects. The science has moved from validating traditional claims to identifying specific mechanisms.

Nigella Sativa significantly reduces blood pressure in hypertensive patients
